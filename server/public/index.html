SentryKey is a self-contained TOTP (two-factor authentication) authenticator for Garmin smartwatches, with Android and iOS companions. Generate codes directly on-device with zero dependencies.

Features Designed for Security

A simple, privacy-respecting structure that places your keys under your control.

⌚

Standalone Watch Authenticator

Rotating 2FA codes are generated entirely on-device on your Garmin watch. No phone is required after your initial sync.

🔐

Zero-Knowledge Cloud Backup

Back up your credentials to your own VPS. All data is encrypted client-side in the browser. The server never sees your raw secrets.

🔄

One-Tap Bluetooth Sync

Add accounts on your phone companion, and push them instantly to your watch over BLE using the Garmin Connect IQ SDK.
